Custom Software FAQ

What is custom software development?

Custom software development means building a digital system specifically for your business workflow, instead of forcing your process into a generic tool.

How do you price custom software?

Pricing is based on scope, features, user roles, database complexity, integrations, and support needs. Custom systems are quoted after discussion.

Can you build a CRM?

Yes. We can build custom CRM systems for leads, clients, follow-ups, sales pipeline, and team management.

Can custom software connect with my website?

Yes. Your website forms, e-commerce store, or landing pages can connect with a custom dashboard or CRM if included in scope.

Will the system be mobile responsive?

Yes. We can design responsive dashboards and portals for desktop and mobile use depending on user needs.
